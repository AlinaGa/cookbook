LEMON GARLIC SHRIMP

Tangy lemon, savory garlic, and tender shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add olive oil and garlic to the skillet and cook. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side. Pour lemon juice over shrimp and stir. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
Notes: You can use pre-cooked shrimp to save time. Goes well with rice or pasta.
