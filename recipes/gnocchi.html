HOMEMADE GNOCCHI

Soft and fluffy potato dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Boil the potatoes until tender, then mash them in a bowl. Add the egg and mix well. Gradually add flour to the mixture until a soft dough forms. Divide the dough into small pieces and roll them into ropes. Cut the ropes into small pieces and press them with a fork to create ridges. Boil the gnocchi in salted water for 2-3 minutes, or until they float to the surface. Serve with your favorite pasta sauce and season with salt and pepper to taste.
