Fried Rice Recipe

Name Description
Spare Ribs Tender and juicy ribs with a sweet and tangy glaze
Time: 2.5hrs
Servings: 5-6
Ingredients:
  • 2 racks of pork spare ribs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
Instructions:
  • Preheat the oven to 300째F (150째C). Season the ribs generously with salt and pepper.
  • Wrap each rack of ribs in foil and place them on a baking sheet. Bake for 2 to 2 1/2 hours, until the meat is tender and almost falling off the bone.
  • While the ribs are baking, make the glaze. In a small saucepan, whisk together the brown sugar, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, rice vinegar, ginger, and garlic. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  • When the ribs are done, remove them from the oven and increase the oven temperature to 425째F (220째C). Carefully unwrap the ribs and brush them generously with the glaze.
  • Return the ribs to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes, until the glaze is bubbly and caramelized.
  • Let the ribs rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving with extra glaze on the side. Enjoy!
