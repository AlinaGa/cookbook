Fried Rice Recipe

Simple and flavorful rice stir-fry with veggies and eggs

Time: 25mins
Servings: 4-5
1 Ingredients:
  • 3 cups cooked white rice, chilled
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
2 Instructions:
  • Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the bottom of the pan.
  • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  • Add the frozen mixed vegetables and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, until they are cooked through and tender.
  • Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then mix them with the vegetables.
  • Add the chilled rice to the pan and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  • Drizzle soy sauce over the rice and stir-fry for another minute or so, until the rice is evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve hot as a side dish or a main course. Enjoy!
